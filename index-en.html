What Trader AI is — and how it works

In 4 steps, from the signal to public verification.

1

The AI reads the market in real time

With each new candle, our neural network with 6 million parameters processes price, volume, volatility, and technical indicators for BTC, ETH, BNB, SOL, and more.

2

It generates a timestamped forecast

The model decides: will the price go up or down in the next interval? That forecast is recorded immediately with date, time, and confidence level — before the result happens.

3

You receive the signal on Telegram

When confidence is high, the signal arrives in the VIP group: asset, direction (buy or sell), time horizon, and estimated target price. 1–2 signals per day, no spam.

4

Accuracy is verified publicly

When the horizon closes, we compare the forecast with the real Binance price and update the public data. Nothing is deleted. You can see the full history in the audit dashboard.

⚠️ Transparency: Trader AI predicts price direction — it does not guarantee profit. Accuracy of 60–68% means the AI is correct more often than not, but losses are still possible. Always use risk management and trade only what you can afford to lose.
